🐾 Personality
😴 Mostly low energy — happy to chill, but watch out for the zoomies if he gets hyped up!
🦴 Tug of war champ — not a fetch dog, but he will go hard on tug. Great way to tire him out.
🍗 Food motivated — grunts on schedule for meals. Don't skip or he'll let you know.
🛋️ Furniture allowed — he's allowed on the couch and bed, no need to keep him off.
🛏️ Sleeps in his own bed — put his bed right next to yours at night. He needs to be near his people but will settle in his own spot.
🤗 Affectionate & people-oriented — loves attention and cuddles.
🛋️🙋 Asks permission for furniture — he'll look at you before jumping up. Just say yes or no, he'll respect it.
🚪 Tells you when he needs to go out — he'll bark at the door. Don't ignore it!
😏 Gloating = he wants you — if he's prancing around looking pleased with himself, he wants attention, play, or just to be glued to your side.
